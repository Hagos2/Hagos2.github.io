Popularity of CompSci at MIU

During the 2019-2020 academic year, Maharishi International University handed out 7 bachelor's degrees in computer science. Due to this, the school was ranked #34 in popularity out of all colleges and universities that offer this degree. This is an increase of 75% over the previous year when 4 degrees were handed out.

In 2020, 390 students received their masters degree in compsci from MIU. This makes it the #2 most popular school for compsci masters degree candidates in the country.

MIU Computer Science Bachelors Program

The compsci program at MIU awarded 7 bachelor's degrees in 2019-2020. About 71% of these degrees went to men with the other 29% going to women.

The majority of the bachelor's degree graduates for this major are black or African Americans. About 71% of grads fell into this category.
